Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ today }}

1. What we collect

To provide the Service we collect: the vehicle registration number you enter, your phone number, the location you book the cleaning at, an anonymous device identifier, and (optionally) your email address if you save one in Settings.

2. How we use it

We use your data to schedule and deliver the cleaning, to contact you about your booking, to send a booking confirmation email if you provided one, and to operate and improve the Service.

3. Sharing

We share data with: (a) the cleaner assigned to your booking, limited to what they need to perform the service; (b) infrastructure providers (database hosting, email delivery) acting on our behalf. We do not sell your personal data.

4. Retention

Order records are retained for up to 24 months to support refunds, disputes and accounting. You can request deletion at any time using the contact email below; some data may be retained where required by law.

5. Your rights

Under the EU GDPR you have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, port, or object to processing of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights contact privacy@cleencar.example.

6. Security

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS) and standard access controls on our database. No system is perfectly secure; please contact us immediately if you suspect a security issue.

7. Cookies / local storage

Our mobile app stores small amounts of data on your device (your saved plate, phone, email) so the app can pre-fill forms. This data never leaves your device unless you place a booking that uses it.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions: privacy@cleencar.example.
