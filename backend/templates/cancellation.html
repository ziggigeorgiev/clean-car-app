Cancellation Policy

Last updated: {{ today }}

1. Free cancellation window

You may cancel any booking free of charge up to 2 hours before its scheduled start time.

2. Late cancellations

If you cancel within 2 hours of the start time, a fee of 50% of the booking total applies. This covers reserved slot time and the cleaner's travel.

3. No-shows

If the cleaner arrives at the agreed location but cannot start the service (vehicle unavailable, no access, etc.), the full booking price will be charged.

4. We cancel

If we have to cancel your booking — e.g. extreme weather, vehicle/equipment issue, or unavailable cleaner — you pay nothing. We will offer to reschedule and, where possible, provide a discount on your next booking.

5. How to cancel

Open the booking in the app and use the cancel option, or contact us at support@cleencar.example.

6. Refunds

Refunds for already-paid bookings are processed to the original payment method within 5 business days.
